409 Conflict

integrity-violation

A database integrity constraint was violated.

Common Causes

  • Foreign key references a non-existent record
  • Check constraint failed (value out of allowed range)
  • Not-null constraint on a required field
  • Attempting to delete a record with dependent references

Resolution

Ensure all referenced records exist before creating relationships. When deleting, remove dependent records first or use cascade operations where appropriate.
